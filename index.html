Pacific Maritime

The Pacific Maritime lies along the west coast of Canada. Home to a wide veriety of animals as well as the mighty costal mountains. Some of the largest cities in Canada like Victoria and Vancouver are located in the Pacific Maritime and with populations in the millions.

Tourism

This spectacular environment is perfect for a variety of fun outdoor activities like biking, hiking, camping and kayaking, in the summer. Our steep mountains pose a challenge even for most adept skiers and snowboarders. But many come here to embrace the beauty of our Costal Mountains.

Coast Mountains

The Coast mountains are a long mountain range that cover most of the Pacific Maritime. Like the name suggests, the Coast Mountain are located at the west coast of Canada facing the Pacific Ocean. These mountains are tall, with rugged slopes and jagged peaks covered with snow. The mountains are magnificent but deadly.
