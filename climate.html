Climate

The Pacific Maritime is considered quite mild compared to most of Canada and is nice to live in the winter and the summer.The temperature is milder with the average temperature in the summer 13 degrees Celsius and in the winter -1.5 degrees Celsius. It is the wettest place in Canada with some places receiving around 4000 mm of precipitation.
